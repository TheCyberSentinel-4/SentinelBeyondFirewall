Promtail (Ship logs)
	- Promtail is an agent that tails log files and pushes them to Loki.
	- Promtail can be configured to collect logs from various sources 
	such as local files, systemd, or Docker containers, and then send them to Loki.

Graffana (Visualize And Monitor Logs)
	- Query and Visualize logs from Loki

Loki (Store and Query logs)
	- Log Storage area, sent by Promtail!
